About

Hi, I'm Zack.

I hold a Virginia Secondary Mathematics teaching license and a Master of Education from Marymount University. Before entering the classroom, I spent two decades in consulting, marketing and analytics — so I know how what it means to think through tough problems and what it takes to be successful.

I've taught Math 7 and Math 8 in Falls Church City Public Schools, and I tutor students across grades 6–9 in the Arlington and NoVA area. My goal is to help your student improve while feeling more confident in math class.
